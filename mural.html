BETTER TOGETHER

This project brought the company ideals of inclusion, empathy, and community onto the walls themselves.

I was given nearly free reign over one of the halls in GitHub's new wing with instructions to make it reflect the company culture. I settled with the phrase Better Together. Truly, it's the community that makes GitHub what it is. I wanted to represent the distributed workforce, the culture of inclusion, and the idea that none of this would be possible without the beautiful chaos of millions of people working together.
